#  1. Hannah Wilson – Item Owner (Streamer & Influencer)
![Persona 1](Images/Personas/persona1.jpg)

## Scenario A — Register a Videogame and Manage Availability
Hannah receives the game *Animal Crossing* from a sponsor and wants to list it for rent.

**Steps**
1. Hannah creates an account and logs in as a User.
2. She opens the **Owner Dashboard**.  
3. Clicks **“Add New Item”** to register a videogame.  
4. Fills in the game details:  
   - Title  
   - Description  
   - Photos  
   - Condition  
   - Rental price per day  
5. Sets availability (dates, active/inactive).  
6. Publishes the listing so renters can find it.  
7. Receives booking requests and approves/declines them.  
8. Tracks payments, shipping, and returns within the dashboard.

**Goal:** Monetize the games she receives by renting them out.

---

## [*EXTENDED*] Scenario B — View Revenue and Videogame Analytics 
Hannah wants to understand how her games are performing.

**Steps**
1. Opens the analytics section in the Owner Dashboard.  
2. Views indicators such as:  
   - Monthly revenue  
   - Most rented items  
   - Occupancy rate per item  
3. Adjusts prices or availability based on performance data.

**Goal:** Optimize revenue and item performance.

---

## [*EXTENDED*] Scenario C — Communicate With Renters
Hannah needs to clarify details with renters.

**Steps**
1. Receives a message from a renter in the platform’s chat system.  
2. Exchanges details about shipping, conditions, or return instructions.  
3. Updates listing information if needed.

**Goal:** Ensure smooth rental processes and reduce misunderstandings.

# 2. Tom Schmidt – Renter (Bartender & 3D Maker)
![Persona 2](Images/Personas/persona2.jpg)

## Scenario A — Search, Discover, and Book a Videogame
Tom wants to rent a story-driven RPG without buying it.

**Steps**
1. Registers and logs in as a User.  
2. Uses the search bar to find games.  
3. Applies filters (platform, genre, price range, owner rating).  
4. Selects *The Legend Of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom* from search results.  
5. Chooses rental period and checks availability.  
6. Confirms booking.  
7. Completes payment.  
8. Receives booking confirmation in the **Renter Dashboard**.

**Goal:** Access the games he wants for a lower cost.

---

## Scenario B — Manage Active and Past Rentals
Tom wants to keep track of his rental activity.

**Steps**
1. Opens the **Renter Dashboard**.  
2. Views:  
   - Active rentals  
   - Remaining days  
   - Delivery and return status  
3. Reviews past rentals in the history tab.  
4. Receives automated notifications for return deadlines.

**Goal:** Stay organized and avoid delays or late fees.

---

## [*EXTENDED*] Scenario C — Advanced Search and AI Recommendations
Tom wants personalized suggestions based on his preferences.

**Steps**
1. Platform analyzes Tom’s rental history and preferences.  
2. Generates AI-powered recommendations for similar games.  

**Goal:** Quickly discover relevant games without extensive searching.

#  3. Ronan – Renter (Father & QA Engineer)
![Persona 3](Images/Personas/persona3.jpg)
## Scenario A — Rent Family-Friendly Games for Weekend Play (MVP)
Ronan wants to find a family-friendly game to play with his two sons.

**Steps**
1. Registers and logs in as a User.  
2. Uses the search bar and applies filters such as age rating and multiplayer mode.
3. Selects a family-friendly game like *Mario Kart*.
4. Books the videogame for the weekend.  
5. Completes payment.  
6. Tracks rental status through the dashboard.

**Goal:** Create bonding moments through gaming.

---

## [*EXTENDED*] Scenario B — Pre/Post Rental Condition Checks
Ronan appreciates clarity and transparency regarding item condition.

**Steps**
1. Before returning a game, fills out a condition checklist.  
2. Uploads photos of the game’s condition.  
3. Submits the post-rental condition report.

**Goal:** Maintain trust and avoid disputes.

---

## [*EXTENDED*] Scenario C — Notifications & Messaging
Ronan needs help coordinating pickups or schedules.

**Steps**
1. Receives notifications for booking approvals and reminders.  
2. Uses in-app messaging to contact the owner.  
3. Arranges return logistics based on his family schedule.

**Goal:** Keep rentals organized despite a busy routine.

#  4. Dawan Houtcheques – Admin
![Persona 4](Images/Personas/persona4.jpg)
## Scenario A — Monitor Overall Platform Operations
Dawan wants to ensure the platform runs smoothly and reliably, showing his capability to manage complex systems effectively.

**Steps**
1. Logs in to the backoffice dashboard as an **Admin**.  
2. Reviews system health indicators such as server uptime, load, and response times.  
3. Detects any anomalies or alerts.  
4. Escalates issues to technical teams or takes corrective action.

**Goal:** Maintain platform stability and demonstrate his technical competence.

---

## Scenario B — Manage User Accounts and Partnerships
Dawan wants to keep the platform safe and maintain trust among users.

**Steps**
1. Reviews new user registrations and verifies accounts.  
2. Approves or flags accounts based on compliance checks.  
3. Manages partnerships with owners or influencers.  
4. Resolves disputes or account issues when necessary.

**Goal:** Ensure a safe and well-functioning marketplace.

---

## Scenario C — Analyze Platform Metrics and KPIs
Dawan wants to track performance and identify areas for improvement.

**Steps**
1. Opens the analytics dashboard.  
2. Reviews key metrics: active users, listings, bookings, and transaction volumes.  
3. Identifies trends, bottlenecks, or underperforming areas.  
4. Prepares reports to support data-driven decisions.

**Goal:** Improve platform efficiency and business outcomes.

---

## Scenario D — Ensure Compliance and Safety Standards
Dawan wants to maintain legal and platform policy compliance, demonstrating responsibility and professionalism.

**Steps**
1. Reviews platform activity and listings for policy violations.  
2. Flags or removes non-compliant content.  
3. Coordinates with security or legal teams if needed.  
4. Updates internal procedures to maintain high standards.

**Goal:** Keep the platform trustworthy, safe, and compliant, while showing strong governance skills.
